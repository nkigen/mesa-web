Mighty Eagles F.C/ Soccer Academy

Mighty Eagles F.C/ Soccer academy located in Kitale, Transnzoia County at JKUAT Kitale Campus grounds, formerly Robinsons Academy. We are dedicated to developing young soccer players into skilled and confident athletes. Our soccer academy offers a comprehensive training program that is designed to provide your child with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed on the field. We have a team of experienced coaches who are committed to helping your child reach their full potential as a soccer player.
