MESA

All are welcomed

MESA is located in Kitale, Transnzoia County at JKUAT Kitale Campus grounds, formerly Robinsons Academy. We are dedicated to developing young soccer players into skilled and confident athletes. Our soccer academy offers a comprehensive training program that is designed to provide your child with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed on the field. We have a team of experienced coaches who are committed to helping your child reach their full potential as a soccer player.

At MESA, we believe that proper training is essential for young footballers. Not only does it help them improve their skills and technique, but it also ensures that they stay safe on the field. Football is a physically demanding sport, and young players are more susceptible to injuries if they don't receive proper training.
