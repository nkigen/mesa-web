Join our soccer academy located in Kitale, Transnzoia County at JKUAT Kitale Campus grounds, formerly Robinsons Academy. We are dedicated to developing young soccer players into skilled and confident athletes. Our soccer academy offers a comprehensive training program that is designed to provide your child with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed on the field. We have a team of experienced coaches who are committed to helping your child reach their full potential as a soccer player.

Why chose the Eagles?

As a parent or guardian, you may have noticed that your child has a keen interest in football. They might spend hours practicing their dribbling, shooting, and passing skills. However, if you want your child to succeed as a footballer, it's important to ensure that they receive proper training. At Mighty Eagles Academy, we believe that proper training is essential for young footballers. Not only does it help them improve their skills and technique, but it also ensures that they stay safe on the field. Football is a physically demanding sport, and young players are more susceptible to injuries if they don't receive proper training. Our experienced coaches use a range of training techniques to help young players develop their skills. From agility drills to ball control exercises, our training sessions are designed to improve players' overall game. We also place a strong emphasis on teamwork, communication, and sportsmanship, all of which are essential for success on the field. By enrolling your child in our academy, you can rest assured that they will receive the best possible training. Our coaches are passionate about football and are committed to helping young players reach their full potential. So if you want to give your child the best possible chance of success in football, consider enrolling them in Mighty Eagles Academy. If you think you have what it takes to be a Mighty Eagle, we invite you to join us for tryouts. Our coaches will be on hand to assess your skills, and give you feedback on your performance. We are looking for players who are dedicated, have a passion for the game, and who want to take their game to the next level. Date: Weekends Time: 1:00 PM - 5:30 PM Location: JKUAT grounds Kitale, Trans-Nzoia.

Dear esteemed viewers, We hope this finds you well. We are writing to express our interest in seeking your support for Mighty Eagles Football Academy, located in Kitale, Kenya, as we look to attend the prestigious Gothia Super Cup in Sweden. Mighty Eagles Football Academy was established with the aim of providing young footballers in Kitale with a platform to showcase their skills and compete against the best in the world. Our academy has been making great strides in developing young talent, and attending the Gothia Super Cup would be a huge milestone for us. The Gothia Super Cup is one of the largest and most prestigious youth football tournaments in the world, attracting top teams from around the globe. It is a unique opportunity for our young players to compete against some of the best young talent in the world, and to learn and grow from the experience. However, attending the Gothia Super Cup is not without its challenges. The cost of travel, accommodation, and equipment can be prohibitively expensive, and this is where we are hoping for your support. Your assistance would go a long way in helping us make this dream a reality for our young players. We believe that the experience of competing in the Gothia Super Cup would be invaluable for our young players, both in terms of their development as footballers and as individuals. We hope that the county Government of Trans Nzoia will support this team to honor their invitation to the Gothia Cup tournament in Gothenburg Sweden July this year. It would also help raise the profile of our academy, and of football in Kitale more broadly. Thank you for considering our request.
